I'm Not Okay

Sleep:

Difficulty sleeping through the night, working early, eating too much

Physical:

Decreased energy, greater fatigue, changes in appetite, weight changes, aches, pain, headaches, increased cramps

Emotional:

A feel of worthlessness or guilt, emptiness, sadness and hopelessness. Fixating on past failures and self-blame

Behavioral:

Loss of interest, no longer finding pleasure in favorite activities, feeling tired easily, thoughts of suicide, drinking excessively, using drugs, engaging in high-risk activities

Mood:

Anger, aggressiveness, irritability, anxiousness, restlessness

Exercice:

Improve strength, prevent aging, develop muscles, increase self-esteem, reduce stress and anxiety

Communication:

Reduce stress, ask for help when needed, enhance confidence and Express Your feelings

Relaxation:

Reduce muscle tension , chronic pain , anger and frustration , improve concentration and mood

Self-Acceptance:

Love Yourself, Body image positivity, Accept failures to embrace your future

Be Kind:

Feel needed and valued, Boosts self-esteem, Improve your own happiness and other's, build authenticity and humility
